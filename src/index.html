Responsive Eye is a multimedia exhibition in three parts by Brooklyn artist PETER BURR.

Drawing upon the history of minimalist painting and op-art, the works in this show explore the limits of human perception by staging a series of optical illusions which highlight the visceral intensities of distorting occlusions which puncture our awareness. Through the dissonant juxtaposition of artificial human bodies and technologically driven abstractions, a portrait emerges of an anxious divided society, pushing at the boundaries of our teetering attention.
